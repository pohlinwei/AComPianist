AComPianist

AComPianist is a computer pianist that plays an accompanying piece when an image is uploaded.

Upload an image and hover over it to influence the generated music.

Start

Created with Magenta's Performance RNN and a simple emotion classification neural network. All code and references can be found here.

Upload a .jpeg with a minimum dimension of 256px by 256px.

Then, hover your mouse over the image to influence the generated music.

Or just sit back and enjoy!
